Suggestion 6
Suggestions:

1. It is important  for every person to take Indigo survey as this shows individual strengths, weakness and preferences. This results to increased self awareness including self control, higher creativity, self-esteem, better able to break bad behaviors, better goal achievement and better relationships.

2. Students should understand the DISC model as this will help them succeed. It is a great way for them to see their own communication style and learn about others. They should also practice some changes to their behavioral style. If they do that, they could see that it makes a big difference in  school and future success.
